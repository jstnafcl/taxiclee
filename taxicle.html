Your Safety is Our Priority

We have built multiple layers of safety into every single ride on Taxicle.

Background-Checked Drivers

Every Taxicle driver undergoes a thorough background check, NBI clearance verification, and community screening before they can accept rides.

Live GPS Tracking

Your trip is tracked in real-time via GPS from start to finish. Share your ride status with family or friends in one tap.

Emergency SOS Button

An in-app SOS button instantly alerts our safety team and shares your location if you ever feel unsafe during a ride.

Ratings and Reviews

After every ride, passengers rate their experience. Drivers with consistently low ratings are suspended from the platform.

Secure Data and Payments

Your personal data and payment information are encrypted and never shared with third parties. Your privacy is protected at all times.

24/7 Support Team

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Taxicle.

How do I book a ride? ▼

Simply download the Taxicle app, enter your pickup location and destination, and tap "Book Now". A nearby driver will be matched with you instantly!

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept both cash and GCash payments. You can choose your preferred payment method before booking your ride.

Are all drivers verified? ▼

Yes! All Taxicle drivers undergo thorough background checks and must have valid licenses. We also verify their tricycle registration and insurance.

How is the fare calculated? ▼

Fares are based on distance and follow the local tricycle fare matrix. The exact fare is shown before you confirm your booking, so there are no surprises!

Can I track my driver? ▼

Absolutely! Once matched, you can see your driver's location in real-time on the map and get an accurate ETA.

What payment methods are accepted? ▼

Taxicle accepts both cash and GCash. You can select your preferred payment method when booking a ride. More payment options may be added in future updates.

Which areas does Taxicle serve? ▼

Taxicle currently operates across all 31 barangays of Dagupan City, Pangasinan — including Tondaligan, the City Plaza, Magsaysay Market, PSU, and all surrounding communities.

How do I share my trip with someone? ▼

During an active ride, tap the "Share Trip" button in the app to send your live location and trip details to a contact via SMS or messaging apps. This is great for keeping family informed about your whereabouts.

What do I do if I left something in a tricycle? ▼

Go to your ride history in the app and tap "Report Lost Item" on the relevant trip. Our support team will contact the driver on your behalf. You can also reach us directly through the Help Center.

Is Taxicle available 24/7? ▼

Driver availability depends on active drivers in your area at any given time. The app shows you real-time driver availability. During peak hours, drivers are most abundant. Our support team is available 24/7 for assistance.
